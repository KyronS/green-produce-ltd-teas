Lemongrass Tea

Experience the refreshing and invigorating taste of our Lemongrass Tea. Known for its bright citrus notes and subtle sweetness, lemongrass offers a delightful brew that is both soothing and revitalizing. This tea is perfect for those seeking a calming beverage that aids digestion and promotes relaxation. Enjoy it hot or iced, and let the natural zest of lemongrass lift your spirits and refresh your mind.

Bay Leaf Tea

Discover the aromatic and robust flavor of our Bay Leaf Tea. Renowned for its distinctive herbal profile, bay leaf tea provides a rich, earthy taste with hints of subtle spice. This traditional beverage is cherished for its potential health benefits, including digestive support and anti-inflammatory properties. Savor a warm cup of bay leaf tea and embrace its comforting essence and wellness-enhancing qualities.

Bay Leaf & Cinnamon Tea

Indulge in the harmonious blend of our Bay Leaf & Cinnamon Tea. This exquisite combination marries the earthy tones of bay leaves with the warm, spicy notes of cinnamon, creating a truly captivating brew. Known for its soothing and aromatic qualities, this tea is perfect for unwinding after a long day or adding a touch of warmth to your routine. Enjoy the balance of flavors that not only delights the palate but also offers numerous health benefits.

Sorrel & Ginger Tea

Experience the vibrant and tangy fusion of our Sorrel & Ginger Tea. This dynamic duo combines the tart, fruity flavor of sorrel with the zesty, spicy kick of ginger, resulting in a bold and invigorating tea. Sorrel and ginger are both celebrated for their immune-boosting and anti-inflammatory properties. Enjoy this tea hot or cold, and let its refreshing taste and healthful benefits rejuvenate your senses.

Turmeric Golden Milk Drops

Embrace the golden warmth of our Turmeric Golden Milk Drops. Known for its earthy and slightly peppery flavor, turmeric tea is a powerful blend that offers a wealth of health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. This vibrant tea is perfect for those looking to enhance their wellness routine with a natural and nourishing beverage. Sip on a cup of turmeric tea and let its rich, comforting taste and health-boosting qualities enrich your day.!

Annatto Seeds Tea

Delve into the unique and vibrant flavor of our Annatto Seeds Tea. Annatto seeds, known for their bright orange hue and subtle, nutty taste, create a distinctive and delightful brew. This tea is not only visually appealing but also packed with antioxidants and potential health benefits, including support for eye health and anti-inflammatory properties. Enjoy a cup of annatto seeds tea and experience a truly exotic and beneficial addition to your tea collection.
